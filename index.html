Instructions

  1. First keep laptop screen straight
  2. move yourself 3-4 feet away from laptop
  3. Move your right wrist in acending and desending
  4. Now press play button and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from laptop
  5. the red paddle is your paddle that will allow movement as per your movement of your right wrist
